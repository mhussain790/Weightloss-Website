1 Lifechanging way to Lose Weight!

We're experimenting with differrent article layouts so we'll keep this one short and direct.

What's an easy way anyone can do in under 10 minutes to lose weight?

Drink Water and only Water! Drinks like Soda, Juice, Energy drinks, etc. have calories in the form of sugar. Let's take a look at the nutrition label of a 20 Fl Oz bottle of Sprite:

The bottle has one serving with a total of 240 Calories. Each servings has 64 grams of Sugar which basically means it has 64 grams of Carbs. If, for example, 1 cup of rice has about 35 to 40 grams of Carbs then drinking one soda is equal to about eating 1 and a half cups of rice.

Orange Juice has about 110 calories per serving, which contains 26 grams of Carbs and 2 grams of Protein. Don't get us wrong here. Orange Juice has a lot of health benefits and vitamins that it carries. One thing you should remember is that many commercial Orange Juice brands add a lot more sugar which increases the number of calories in as well.

But how often do you actually drink only one or two servings?

Whenever you pull out a carton of Orange Juice or a big Liter of Coke, do you also get a measuring cup to see how many servings you're drinking? Many people don't measure how much they're actually drinking. So you'll be taking in a lot more Calories by doing this.
