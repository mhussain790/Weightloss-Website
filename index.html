What is the fastest way to lose fat?

The fastest way to lose fat is to be consistent. Being serious for a week or two then eating fast food 3-4 times on your third week won’t help at all. People generally give up or forget about their diet/exercise, which is why the fastest way to lose weight is consistency. Time will change things.

What is the best way lose fat or weight?

Losing weight, specifically fat, is not complicated at all. It mainly consists of nutrition and exercise. The best way to lose weight and fat from your body is to modify your calories. Basically, you lower your calories to lose weight and increase them to gain weight or muscle. Although, you can't just eat a ridiculously low amount of calories because your body will eat muscle, and possibly gain fat instead. Furthermore, there is one more factor that is more important than either and that’s you! Many people want to lose weight but they end up giving up or their old habits kick in. We cover nutrition and exercise, but we also talk about creating healthy habits that have a greater impact than everything else.
