Exercise

Nutrition is very important. In order to lose weight we need to be in a CALORIC DEFICIT. To gain weight we need to be in a CALORIE SURPLUS. To stay at the same weight we need to be at CALORIC MAINTENANCE.


If we think of fat loss as being in a deficit then it makes the concept of weight loss a lot simpler. We as humans burn calories from almost every physical activity we do. This can be from picking up a cup to turning the pages in a book. Caloric Maintenance is the amount of calories a person needs to eat in one day in order to stay the SAME weight. Let's say this person wanted to lose fat. They would then eat at a Calorie Deficit, which basically means eating a certain amount less than your Caloric Maintenance. What if this person wanted to gain weight? In order to gain weight, they would have to eat at a Caloric Surplus, or eating over their Caloric Maintenance.

We go over how to count calories such as Maintenance, Deficit, and Surplus Calories for your own body and weight. They will also include each Macronutrient amount for Protein, Carbs, and Fats. Click the button below to start counting!

I’ll be using arbitrary or random numbers in order to make the math a bit easier.

Maintenance = 2000

Caloric Deficit = 2000 - 500 = 1500
CALORIES FOR WEIGHT LOSS

Caloric Surplus = 2000 + 500 = 2500
CALORIES FOR WEIGHT GAIN


How does exercise play a role in this?

Think of it like this:

Let's think of our body as a pot on a stove with the burner on low, and the food we eat can be thought of as water added to the pot. The top rim of the pot will be our Calorie Deficit line. If we add too much water to the pot, then the pot will overflow and spill onto the stove top. The burner represents physical exercise during the day. So at times the burner will be very low, low, high, etc. If we exercise for 20 minutes then the burner turns to high, which starts boiling the water. If the water boils for 20 minutes straight then we'll lose some water in the pot from evaporation.
By controlling how much water you add into the pot and using the burner temperature as a way to regulate the pot we can find an equal balance. By finding the balance for our bodies, we can use this to burn fat away. But this only works if you are consistent!

So once again, exercise helps us burn calories in order to maintain a Calorie Deficit. This is the major way we can burn away fat from our bodies! The most common way people do this is through CARDIO. For example, we usually do around 250-300 Calories Burned or around 25 minutes on an Elliptical machine depending on the resistance.

Here is another example: So our Caloric Deficit is 1500 calories, but we accidentally ate 1650 calories that day. If we burn 150 calories doing exercise, specifically through cardio, then we’ll be right at 1500 calories.

Exercise also helps our body in various ways like increasing your cardiovascular health, strength, endurance, etc. There are many accounts of exercise and diet helping performance in bed such as stamina in order to “last longer” and pleasure.
