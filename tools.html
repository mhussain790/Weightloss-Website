Tools for Weight Loss

At Get Fit, we won’t make you buy supplements or useless items you won’t need in your journey. However, there are some tools you’ll need in order to lose weight and keep track of your progress. These are everyday products that we’ve found ourselves using.


  1. Tracking Food

    2. We recommend download the app called MyFitnessPal, which is available on FREE on both Android and iOS. MyFitnessPal is an app that basically counts your calories for you. You can search almost any food in their database that can added to your diary where your calories are tracked. It also tracks Macronutrients, Micronutrients, and Exercise. You can even enter challenges sponsored by companies to win gift cards and other prizes. We recommend this app mainly to count Calories and Macronutrients. If you don’t have a phone or tablet, you can use all the features of the app on their website at www.myfitnesspal.com.


    They do have a feature that helps you count Calories and Macronutrients, but it is an estimate that might not be close to your actual number. You can pay for the premium version for those features, but we go over counting Calories and Macronutrients in the Nutrition section.


  2. Weighing Food with a Scale

    3. Our next recommendation is purchasing a food scale. Even though this will cost you some money, a food scale will take accurate measurements of the serving sizes of food you’re eating in order for you to hit your Calorie/Macronutrient goals. Measuring cups or spoons won’t accurately weigh food or liquid. You might think the marks are accurately weighing a serving size, but it might actually be a few servings. We use ours everyday to measure our meals so it is money well-spent. You can find them at Walmart or Target for $20 to $30.

  3. Shaker Bottle

    4. If you’re going to be using Protein Powder as a shake then this will be a cheap expense. We recommend buying a Shaker Bottle. Having to use a blender for shakes 2 or even 3 times a day can be rough. Cleaning a blender multiple times a day, everyday, will take a good chunk of your time. On the other hand, Shaker Bottles are easy to clean, fairly inexpensive, and you can find them online from $7 to $12.

    We don’t recommend spending much money on one. They’re made of plastic so you won’t have to worry about them breaking and even the shaker ball inside won’t get ruined very easily. Shaker bottles can smell pretty horrible if you leave them in your gym bag or car, but as long as you wash them regularly then you should be fine. If you ever need to replace them then another $7 won’t put too much of a dent in your wallet.

  4. Plastic Containers or Tupperware

    5. Once you’ve figured out how many Calories and Macronutrients you’ll need to eat everyday to lose weight, you might start Meal Prepping. Meal prep is when you make you meals in advance, maybe over the weekend or the day before. Everyone does it differently. Some people make their meals for the whole week while others just do it for the next day or two. If you’re going to be meal prepping or even taking lunch to work then we recommend some type of plastic container or tupperware. By using plastic containers, your meals are ready to go with you to work or on vacation. You’ll want to find Microwave Safe and BPA/Toxin-free tupperware. They’re available at virtually any grocery store or at your local WalMart/Target. However, it might be expensive to buy 7 to 10 containers at stores.
