Goals and Planning

What's your goal?


1. Choose an End Goal:

Before we start our journey, we need to figure out what our goals are, and create a plan that will help us reach our goal successfully. I recommend writing these down on a piece of paper and taping it up somewhere where you’ll see it everyday.

First, we need to decide on what we want to do in order to set our end goals.

  1. Do you want to lose weight/fat?
    2.
  2. Do you want to gain weight/muscle?
    3.

Let’s say we choose option 1. From here, we need to continue to narrow it down to create an end goal. If we want to lose weight then how much do you want to lose? Your end goal can be reaching a certain body fat %, losing 20-30 pounds, or just to look good. Remember, you can have as many end goals as you want. We’re just using one as an example.

Ex:

End Goal: I want to Lose 30 lbs.

End Goal: I want to be able to do 20 pullups.

2. Choose a Short Term Goal:

Jumping into something solely driven by motivation will actually lead you to failure. We want to ease ourselves into the process. If you decide to lose weight and change your diet completely the next day, you’re going to have a very difficult time adjusting.


By slowly easing into things we can start creating good habits. For example, waking up early, not going to McDonald’s at lunch, weighing out your food, etc. We do this by setting short term or smaller goals that we can finish in a week or two.

For instance, we can set a short term goal such as going to the gym twice in Week 1, making your own food for 3 days, or waking up early the next day. These goals are like a checklist for the next week or for every week. By accomplishing each short term goal, you’re getting the satisfaction of completing the task and creating a healthy habit. We believe that’s one of the main things people miss when trying to complete this journey.

Ex:

Short Term Goal #1: I want to lose 3 lbs by the end of week 2.

Short Term Goal #2: Go to the Gym 3 times this week!

Short Term Goal #3: Walk for 10 minutes outside on Wednesday.
